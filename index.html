Why this summit exists now.

AI is becoming industrial, physical and global at the same time. GO Summit gives founders, investors and operators a practical way to understand China and Asia from inside the rooms where builders are actually working.

Market signal

China is where AI meets supply-chain reality.

Models, robotics, EV supply chains, embodied AI and advanced manufacturing are moving too quickly to understand from the outside.

Bridge

AGI Villa connects builders across borders.

The platform brings Chinese founders, international operators, ecosystem leaders and global capital into the same long-term working relationship.

Format

Each stop is built for work.

The format combines stage content, closed-door roundtables, investor access, local market context and company visits.

Five questions every AI founder must answer before going global.

The program is built around the practical decisions behind global expansion: product, supply chain, infrastructure, capital, market access and operating playbooks.

Track 01

Cross-border GTM

Market entry, localization, overseas operations, distribution channels and the operating lessons behind cross-border growth.

Track 02

Robotics & Physical AI

Embodied AI, humanoids, industrial automation, EV supply chains and the manufacturing stack behind real-world intelligence.

Track 03

Infrastructure for AI Products

Compute, model deployment, data systems, agent infrastructure and the technical foundations needed to scale AI products.

Track 04

Capital & Ecosystem Access

Founder-investor matching, strategic capital, corporate innovation, policy signals and ecosystem access across Asia.

Track 05

Founder Operating Playbooks

Operating stories from builders: product-market fit, hiring, pricing, sales, partnerships and the hard calls behind category creation.

Designed for meetings, not just stage time.
