For sponsors.

Three ways to participate

Sponsorship is invite-and-application based. We protect a low sponsor-to-attendee ratio so every conversation has context. Detailed packages, deliverables and pricing are shared via deck on request.

Series Sponsor

Annual sponsor

Year-long presence across all 9 stops. Highest depth of integration: keynote slot, dedicated roundtable, founder-curated 1:1s, year-round content collaboration. Reserved for one or two sponsors per cycle.

Tour Sponsor

3–5 stops, your pick

Choose a thematic or geographic cluster — e.g. the China stops, the Southeast-Asia stops, the hardware-heavy stops. Lighter than Series, deeper than single-stop. Best for sponsors with a regional focus.

Stop Sponsor

One stop, one city

Sponsor a single city. Ideal entry point — taste the format, meet the cohort, decide what comes next. Limited slots per stop to keep the room balanced.

For speakers.

Two ways to take the stage

We program for operator depth, not titles. Most speakers are founders, builders or investors with a hands-on perspective the audience can't easily get elsewhere. Pitches are reviewed on a rolling basis.

Series Speaker

A theme, on tour

Carry one topic across 3–5 stops, building a thread the cohort follows from city to city. Best for builders with a distinct, evolving body of work and bandwidth to travel.

Stop Speaker

One talk, one city
